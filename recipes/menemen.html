Menemen Recipe

Recipes

Description

Menemen is a traditional Turkish egg dish. It is made with eggs, tomatoes, and green peppers. It is cooked in a pan and eaten hot, usually with bread.

Ingrediants

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Chop the green pepper and fry it.
  3. Chop the tomatoes and add them to the pan.
  4. Cook until the tomatoes become soft.
  5. Add salt and black pepper.
  6. Crack the eggs into the pan.
  7. Mix gently and cook until the eggs are done.
  8. Serve hot.
