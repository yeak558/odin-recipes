Pasta Recipe

Recipes

Description

Pasta is a simple and popular dish. It is made by boiling pasta in water. It is often served with sauce, butter, or cheese.

Ingrediants

  • Pasta (spaghetti or any type)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Tomato sauce (optional)
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Put the pasta into the water.

  5. Drain the pasta.

  7. Add tomato sauce if you want.
  8. Serve with grated cheese.
