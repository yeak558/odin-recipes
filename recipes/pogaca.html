Poğaça Recipe

Recipes

Description

Poğaça is a soft Turkish pastry. It is usually eaten for breakfast or as a snack. It can be filled with cheese, olives, or potatoes.

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 packet baking powder
  • Salt
  • Cheese or olives (optional filling)

Steps

  1. Put flour into a bowl.
  2. Add baking powder and salt.
  3. Add milk, oil, and egg.
  4. Mix and knead into a soft dough.
  5. Take small pieces of dough.
  6. Add cheese or olives inside.
  7. Shape the dough.
  8. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes.
